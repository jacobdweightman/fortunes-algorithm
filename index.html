Fortune's Algorithm


The running of Fortune's algorithm is visualized below. Press any key to advance the algorithm one step. On any particular step, one of two things will happen: either a site will be added to the beach line, or a vertex/edge will be added to the voronoi diagram. Visually, You will see the beach line (the piecewise red curve) move from the top to the bottom of the screen as the algorithm runs, and the voronoi edges (black line segments behind the beach line) appearing as the Voronoi diagram is constructed. In the last step, the infinite edges at the edge of the diagram are added in.
